Why "Kaizera"?

"Kaizera", from my GitHub handle kaizera-dev, is a combination of two words:
  • Kaizen - Japanese philosophy of continuous improvement
  • Kwizera - My last name, which means "faith", "hope" or "trust" in Kinyarwanda

The name reflects my mindset: iterate relentlessly and stay grounded in purpose.
