About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I recently completed a placement year as a Statistical Programming Student at UCB Pharma and will return to the University of Reading for the final year of my BSc in Mathematics. In my first two years, I ranked in the top 4 of my cohort with high first-class marks.

At UCB, I contributed to software supporting statisticians and programmers, including gridify, an R package now on CRAN. I also built Shiny apps used in real meetings to explain model behaviour to non-technical audiences — an experience that showed me the value of clarity and usability alongside code quality.

Outside of work, I enjoy weightlifting, basketball, piano, and astrophysics — the latter actually inspiring the background image of this site! I plan on continuing the never-ending journey of learning, specifically in the realms of data analytics, data science and machine learning while building on the teamwork, documentation, and communication skills I’ve developed through my time at university and UCB.

Why "Kaizera"?

The name Kaizera is a blend of two words: Kaizen, the Japanese principle of continuous improvement, and my surname Kwizera, which means "faith," "hope," or "trust." This combination represents my commitment to ongoing growth, learning, and purposeful work — values I strive to bring to every project and collaboration.
